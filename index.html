About the app

This project is a habit tracker — specifically, a tracker styled for the habits of a beginner programmer, i.e., a junior developer. However, naturally, it can also be used to track other habits, such as health, studying, and so on.

All the icons were taken from free online icon librarie Flaticon — huge thanks to their creators!
